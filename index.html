Limit Gauges

Our Limit Gauge section encompasses gauges like Plain Plug Gauge, Ring Gauge, Snap Gauge, Dial Snap Gauge etc. They are made of special grade Tungsten Carbide / Steel. Custom requirements are also catered.

Air Gauges

Our Air Gauges are indispensable tool for precision batch and mass production. They are of very high accuracy with fast response time. We cater Air Plug Gauge, Air Ring Gauge, Air Snap Gauge of various range.
